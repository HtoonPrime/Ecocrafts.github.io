Why do we reduce plastic/things for waste management?

Reducing plastic waste is crucial for environmental sustainability. Plastic is a non-biodegradable material that remains in our environment for hundreds of years and produces a variety of negative effects. It harms wildlife in the oceans and on land, pollutes our surroundings, and releases harmful chemicals which contribute to global warming and harm human health.

Reducing plastic waste requires changes in daily habits such as consuming less single-use plastic, practicing reuse and recycling, and using eco-friendly alternatives. These small changes can contribute to a significant reduction in environmental pollution, protecting the planet for future generations.
